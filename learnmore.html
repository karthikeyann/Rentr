Rentr!

Choose area, Tour, like a house? Move in.

A common problem for people moving to a new city is finding a good place to live and finally moving in. We provide the user a tour of available apartments in localities he wants to live. The user books slots on our app when he can go around looking for houses and where he wants to live and we take care of everything else. Unlike brokers who charge exorbitant rates, we care for our users (ourselves having gone through this pain) and help him find a great place to live.
